About Me

Hi, I'm Steven

Hi everyone, my name is Steven Cowart and I am kicking off my tech journey here in Savannah, GA. I'm a 22 year old majoring in Information Technology with a focus on Cybersecurity. This portfolio showcases the skills I have learned in my very first major class, Intro to Web Design. Follow me on my journey in building my first functional webpage.

My Approach to Coding

As a beginner in web design, I'm using my portfolio as a testing ground for the skills I learn in class every week. With this hands-on approach, I am watching my code turn from basic pages to more ambitious builds. As my knowledge of coding grows throughout this semester, the boundaries of my portfolio's potential will expand with it. I want my project to serve as an inspiration to fellow aspiring coders. Even though pages seem complex from the outside looking in, it isn't impossible to learn if the time is taken.

Outside of Class
